Privacy Policy — MoodFeedNews
Last updated: May 26, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how the MoodFeedNews mobile application ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.
1. Information we do not collect
The App has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no server of our own. We do not collect, store, or transmit:
Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identity information.
The contents of news articles you read.
Your search queries inside the App.
Precise location (GPS coordinates).
All your preferences — your selected region, blocked keywords, mood-sensitivity setting, source selections, and theme — are stored locally on your device using Android's standard private storage. They never leave your device.
2. Third-party advertising — Google AdMob
The App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob, a service provided by Google LLC. AdMob is independent of us; we do not control it.
To deliver, measure, and prevent fraud in advertising, Google AdMob may collect and process the following from your device:
Advertising identifier (Android Ad ID)
IP address (and the approximate, city-level location derived from it)
Device information (model, OS version, language, time zone)
Information about ads served and your interactions with them (impressions, clicks)
Crash diagnostics related to ad rendering
We never receive this data — it flows directly between your device and Google.
Google's handling of this data is governed by:
Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
You can reset or limit your Android advertising ID at any time: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Delete advertising ID.
3. Consent (EEA, UK, Switzerland)
If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to present a consent form on first launch (and when required by regulation) so you can manage your choices for personalized advertising. Your selection is stored by the consent SDK on your device.
You can change your choice later by clearing the App's data in Android Settings, which will re-trigger the consent form on next launch.
4. Children
The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly process information from children.
5. Security
Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no server-side data to secure. Local data on your device is protected by Android's app sandbox.
6. Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted at the same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
